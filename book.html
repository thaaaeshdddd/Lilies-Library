📅 Book a Session at Lilies Library

✨ Why Book a Session?

Booking a session with Lilies Library gives you a personalized opportunity to get the help you need to succeed. Our sessions are designed to assist secondary students with academic research, study techniques, and using our digital tools effectively. Whether you’re preparing for an exam, starting a research paper, or exploring how to use our online catalog and databases, we are here to guide you.

Examples of sessions you can book:

📌 Example Session Types:
🔹 Research Project Help: Learn how to structure your project, cite sources, and find academic materials.
🔹 Study Skills Coaching: Get tips on time management, note-taking, and exam preparation.
🔹 Digital Library Tools: Learn how to use the OPAC, eBook platforms, and academic databases.
🔹 General Library Tour: A guided introduction to all library services.

Sessions are available in-person or online (via Google Meet or Zoom). You will receive confirmation details by email after booking. Let us help you achieve your learning goals! 🌟
