⚙️ How Lilies Library Works

👤 Step 1: Create Your Free Account

To get started, create your free account by entering your name, email, and choosing a password. This lets you borrow books, save favorites, and receive important updates.

🔎 Step 2: Search Our Online Catalog (OPAC)

Log in and use the search bar to find books, eBooks, journals, and other resources.

  • Search by title, author, or subject.
  • Filter by type or level.
  • Check availability instantly.

📚 Step 3: Borrow or Reserve Books

Found a book? Borrow it immediately if available, or reserve it if someone else has it.

💻 Step 4: Access Digital Resources Anytime

Enjoy instant access to eBooks and academic databases on any device, anywhere.

🧑‍🏫 Step 5: Get Help with Research and Studies

  • Chat with librarians
  • Use citation and study guides
  • Find trusted academic sources

🔔 Step 6: Stay Updated with Notifications

Get reminders before books are due, and alerts for new arrivals or events.

📅 Step 7: Book Personal Sessions with Librarians

Need help with a topic? Book a session for personalized guidance at your convenience.

🌟 Step 8: Join the Library Community

  • Participate in book clubs
  • Recommend new books
  • Give feedback and reviews

🎉 Summary

Lilies Library makes learning simple, fun, and accessible for all students. Explore, learn, and grow with us!
