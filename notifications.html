🔔 Notifications

Stay informed and connected with Lilies Library! 🌟 Our notification system helps you manage your library activities efficiently so you never miss important updates.

You will receive notifications for:

  • 📅 Due Date Reminders – Be notified a few days before your borrowed books are due.
  • ✅ Reservation Ready Alerts – Get informed when your reserved materials are ready.
  • 📨 Overdue Notices – Receive gentle reminders if a return is missed.
  • 🔔 Events & New Arrivals – Stay updated on workshops, talks, and new materials.
📌 Example:
Amir received an SMS reminder that “Advanced Chemistry” was due in 3 days.
Nurul got an email about her reserved book “The Silent Patient” and collected it the same day.

Make sure your contact info is up to date to receive alerts! Visit your Account Page to update email, phone number, or notification preferences.
