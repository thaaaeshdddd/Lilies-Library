📚 Academic Journals & eBooks

Discover a wide range of academic journals and eBooks available through Lilies Library. Our collection covers various disciplines, ensuring students, researchers, and educators have access to the latest and most credible sources.

  • 🔬 STEM Journals: Nature, IEEE Access, Journal of Engineering Education
  • 🎨 Arts & Humanities: Art Journal, The Journal of Aesthetics and Art Criticism
  • 🌍 Social Sciences: Journal of Social Issues, Global Studies Quarterly
  • 📖 Education: Educational Researcher, Journal of Educational Psychology

Our eBook platforms include:

  • 📘 ProQuest eBook Central: Academic titles across disciplines
  • 📗 SpringerLink: Scientific and technical full-text books
  • 📙 Project MUSE: Humanities and social sciences resources
📌 Example:
Sarah used IEEE Access for her robotics project, while Daniel downloaded eBooks from ProQuest for his education thesis. Both found the resources invaluable!

Access all journals and eBooks through our Online Catalog (OPAC). Need help? Contact a librarian for personalized support!
