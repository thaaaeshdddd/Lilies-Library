💾 Digital Resources & Databases

Welcome to Lilies Library’s Digital Resources & Databases hub! 🌐 Our digital collection provides instant access to trusted academic materials, eBooks, e-journals, and research databases. These resources are specially curated to support secondary school students and teachers in their learning, teaching, and research activities.

With 24/7 online availability, you can easily explore diverse subjects — from science and technology to literature and history — anytime, anywhere. Our digital resources offer:

  • 📘 Full-text eBooks covering a wide range of topics
  • 📰 Access to reputable academic journals and magazines
  • 🔍 Searchable databases with peer-reviewed articles
  • 🎥 Educational videos and tutorials
  • 📈 Data sets and research tools for school projects
Example Databases You Can Access:
🔹 Academic Search Complete – Peer-reviewed journal articles in all fields
🔹 ScienceDirect – Scientific articles and book chapters
🔹 ERIC (Education Resources Information Center) – Education-related studies
🔹 eBook Central – Thousands of academic eBooks
🔹 National Geographic Virtual Library – Magazines, maps, and videos

All digital resources are accessible via your Lilies Library login. Use your student ID to sign in and start exploring. If you need guidance, our librarians are ready to assist you through live chat or email. 📧

Empower your learning with Lilies Library’s digital resources — because knowledge should have no boundaries. 🚀
