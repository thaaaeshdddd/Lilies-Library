📖 Online Catalog (OPAC)

Welcome to the Lilies Library OPAC (Online Public Access Catalog)! 🎉 Our OPAC system helps you search, explore, and manage access to thousands of books and academic resources anytime, anywhere. It’s smart, fast, and easy to use!

  • 🔍 Search by title, author, subject, or ISBN
  • 📚 See availability and detailed info instantly
  • 📌 Place holds and reserve books online
  • 🗂️ View your borrowing history and renew books
  • 💡 Get reading suggestions based on your activity
Example Searches:
🔹 Title: The Great Gatsby
🔹 Author: Stephen Hawking
🔹 Subject: Environmental Science
🔹 ISBN: 978-0-123456-78-9

Access our OPAC anytime using your student ID and password. Need help? Use our “Ask a Librarian” chat or contact us for assistance.
