📖 About Lilies Library

🌸 Welcome to Lilies Library! 🌸

Lilies Library is a modern academic library built especially for secondary school students! 🎓 Here, you’ll find books, eBooks, journals, and study tools all in one online place. 📚💻

We created this website as a project for IML470 📊 to support learning in a fun and accessible way. Whether you need homework help, research assistance, or simply love reading, Lilies Library is here for you! 🤗📖

Get ready to explore, discover, and grow your knowledge with us! 🚀🌟
