📅 Book Reservation

Welcome to Lilies Library Book Reservation service! 🌸 Our online reservation system lets you easily reserve academic books, reference materials, and even multimedia resources in advance. No more worrying about availability — secure your materials before you visit the library. 📚✨

With our service, you can:

  • 🔍 Search and locate books, journals, and e-resources from our Online Public Access Catalog (OPAC).
  • 📅 Reserve books for pickup at your preferred date and time.
  • 📬 Receive automatic email notifications when your reserved item is ready for collection.
  • 🧾 View, manage, or cancel your reservations anytime via your personal dashboard.
📌 Example:
Ali reserved “Fundamentals of Physics” for his upcoming exam. He picked it up the next day without any hassle.
Aisyah booked a “Marketing 101” textbook for a group project and was notified as soon as it was ready.

To make a reservation, please ensure that you are logged in to your Lilies Library account. If you don’t have an account yet, contact us to register. Start browsing via our OPAC today and reserve your study essentials in just a few clicks! 🚀
